About UniFriend

Connecting students. Building friendships. Creating communities.

What is UniFriend?

UniFriend is a campus-based social networking platform designed to help university students connect with new friends, study partners, and communities within their campus.

Starting college can be overwhelming. UniFriend makes it easier to find people with similar interests, majors, and goals.

Our Mission

Reduce social barriers in universities and help students build meaningful relationships that support both academic and personal growth.

Our Vision

Create a supportive campus environment where every student feels connected, included, and empowered.
